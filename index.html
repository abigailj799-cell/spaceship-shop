C-193 Starlight Hopper

How to Install Fuel

  1. Open the fuel hatch
  2. Pour or drop in fuel or energy type
  3. Close fuel hatch, or it will all come out
  4. Start the engine and make sure everything is in place
  5. Take off, with doors closed
